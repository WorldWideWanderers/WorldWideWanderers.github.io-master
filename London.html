London

Hello and welcome to our Hometown London! There is so much to see and do in London, it can be quite overwhelming. There are major sights like the Tower of London and Buckingham Palace that are definitely must-sees, but no matter what your interests, the city of London will have something to offer. And don't forget about London's fantastic restaurant and markets scene, a huge variety to choose from! What's there to see? The city offers a variety of sights and sounds. Here are some examples:

Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament

The Elizabeth Tower, commonly known as Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament, are among London's most iconic landmarks. Guided tours can be purchased to experience the Houses of Parliament. The route takes visitors through to the Queen's Robing Room, the Royal Gallery and to the Lords Chamber.

Tower Bridge

Take a walk across this majestic bridge! Inside, there is an exhibition explaining how the world famous bridge works and the history behind its creation. Enjoy the views of London from the walkways situated 45 metres above the Thames.

St Paul's Cathedral

A visit to St Paul's cathedral is a must. The cathedral has an awe-inspiring interior and you can visit the crypt with its tombs. Visitors can try out the Whispering Gallery and continue up to the top of the cathedral and appreciate the amazing views of the city.
